My name is Jennifer Byron and I'm a front-end developer with a passion for bringing digital experiences to life. Like all front-end devs I have studied the arts of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but my vision goes beyond the code and expands into story telling. There are few other places you can invite someone to interact with art and the web is truly one of the greatest platforms to bring it to life. I love to manipluate the power of CSS and build my visions into code. The site you're viewing is all brought to you via CSS. It is truly a masterpiece in it's own right.

I have spent the last 15 years perfecting my craft, pushing boundaries and bringing new ideas to the web community. As a consultant I bring cutting edge techniques and an expert knowledge of front-end design, big data display, and responsive tactics. I am blessed to work tightly with amazing UX minds and together we create stunning prototypes and large scale applications for Fortune 500 clients in a very agile environment; did I mention I'm Scrum certified?

When I'm not coding I spend much of my time drawing, painting, reading, hiking, and photographing the beautiful wilderness of King County.

I'm always working to improve not only my code skills, but soft ones as well through Toast Masters, Women Who Code events, and annual trips to SmashingConf to be sure I'm on top of my game.

"I am an artist, the web is my canvas."
